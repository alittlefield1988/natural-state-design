CANOE RESERVATIONS

Our outfitter uses Buffalo Canoes, which are manufactured right here in Newton County Arkansas (https://buffalocanoemanufacturing.com/)!

Our canoes are available to rent by the day and all prices are plus tax. We also offer shuttling services to the following put-in/take outs on the river. These prices are in addition to the canoe rental price and cover the shuttle cost of one vehicle. You can calculate float distances and times using this site (https://www.nps.gov/buff/planyourvisit/river-accesses-mileage.htm)
