ABOUT US

Welcome to Buffalo River Adventures, where your next adventure is only a click away!

We are located in the heart of the Ozark National Forest and would like to welcome you to come paddle with us down the Buffalo River, stay a while in one of our cozy cabins, or just explore the beauty that is Arkansas!

The Buffalo River became America's First National River on March 1, 1972 and is one of the few remaining free-flowing rivers in the lower forty-eight states. The Buffalo National River is administered by the National Park Service and encompasses 135 miles of its total 150 mile length. These beautiful views in the moutains and valleys make for a stunning getaway destination!

Here are some other activities that are available in the area:

  • Elk Watching
  • Bicycling
  • Hiking
  • Fishing
  • Swimming
  • Dining
  • Education Centers
  • Museums
We are passionate about the outdoors, especially the Buffalo River area. We recently moved here after retirement and are excited to share what we love with you! We have created many memories here at the Buffalo River and we hope to bring the opportunity for everyone else to create their own. We would love to help you create the perfect adventure package for you and your group!
